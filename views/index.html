Where's Joseph Been?

About this
In April 2019 I'm going into the woods to hike the Appalachian trail from GE to ME. With the goal of self reflection and saving memories, I plan to keep a blog here.

Using this blog
Each marker on the map is a place I visited which I decided was important enough to pin and remember. Click on some markers to see what happened there. If a post is very tall, just drag on the map scroll. The most recent post will be open by default. Other than that, this blog is location based instead of chronologically ordered.
